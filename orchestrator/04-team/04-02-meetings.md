# Meetings & Decisions

> MOC: [[00-meta/MOC - Team]]

## Meeting Rhythms (Target)

| Meeting | Frequency | Attendees | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Weekly Standup | Weekly (Mon) | All | Blockers, priorities, wins |
| Monthly Review | Monthly (1st) | All + AI | Metrics review, strategic adjustments |
| Quarterly Planning | Quarterly | Founder + key hires | OKRs, big bets, resource allocation |
| Ad-hoc | As needed | Relevant | Urgent decisions, fires |

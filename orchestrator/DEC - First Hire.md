# DEC — First Hire

## The Problem

You are the bottleneck. You currently do product, ops, sales, marketing, and support. The first hire should remove the biggest constraint.

## Options

| Option | Role | Removes Bottleneck | Risk |
|--------|------|-------------------|------|
| **A. Ops First** | Student Placement Coordinator | Manual lead qualification, viewing scheduling, booking processing | Quality control, training time |
| **B. Dev First** | Full-Stack Engineer | Tech debt, feature velocity, platform scaling | Expensive, may not fix revenue |
| **C. Growth First** | Digital Marketer / Landlord Acquisition | Top of funnel, more leads, more inventory | May not convert if ops is broken |
| **D. Support First** | Support Agent (chat/WhatsApp) | Response time, student satisfaction | Lower impact on revenue |

## Current Lean

**A. Ops First** — if we're moving to service-first differentiation, the human layer is the product.

## Decision Criteria

| Criteria | Weight |
|----------|--------|
| Unblocks revenue fastest | High |
| Reduces founder hours most | High |
| Fits current cash flow | High |
| Strategic alignment | Medium |
| Reversible if wrong | Medium |

# Brand Voice & Identity

> MOC: [[00-meta/MOC - Company]] (brand is part of company identity)

## Brand Personality

**Ten&See is:**
- **Trustworthy** — We verify properties and landlords. No scams, no bait-and-switch.
- **Human** — We talk like a knowledgeable friend, not a corporate chatbot.
- **Helpful** — We anticipate student needs and solve problems before they're asked.
- **Straightforward** — No jargon, no hidden fees, no fine print surprises.

## Tone Guidelines

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use "you" and "we" | Use "the user" or "the client" |
| Be warm and conversational | Be cold or overly formal |
| Be specific ("5-minute walk to Monash") | Be vague ("great location!") |
| Acknowledge concerns honestly | Overpromise or gloss over flaws |
| Use simple language | Use real estate jargon |

## Messaging Examples

### To Students
- ✅ "Found a place you like? We'll help you book it in under 10 minutes."
- ❌ "Submit an enquiry form and our team will process your request."

### To Landlords
- ✅ "Get qualified student tenants without the hassle of screening."
- ❌ "List your property on our platform for maximum exposure."

### WhatsApp Templates
- ✅ "Hey [Name]! Saw you enquired about [Property]. Want me to arrange a viewing this weekend?"
- ❌ "Your enquiry has been received. Reference number: #12345."

## Naming Conventions

- **Product:** "Ten&See" (always ampersand, never "Ten and See")
- **Service tiers:** Basic, Premium, Concierge (avoid "Pro" or "Enterprise")
- **Students:** "Students" or "you" (never "users" or "clients")
- **Landlords:** "Landlords" or "property partners" (never "vendors")

## Content Pillars

2. **Neighborhood Spotlights** — Sunway, Subang, KLCC, etc.
3. **Student Life** — Budgeting, moving tips, roommate advice

# Brand Voice & Identity

## Personality

Ten&See is trustworthy — we verify properties and landlords, with no scams and no bait-and-switch. We are human — we talk like a knowledgeable friend rather than a corporate chatbot. We are helpful — we anticipate student needs and solve problems before they are asked. We are straightforward — no jargon, no hidden fees, no fine print surprises.

## Tone

Use "you" and "we" rather than "the user" or "the client." Be warm and conversational, not cold or overly formal. Be specific — say "five-minute walk to Monash" rather than "great location." Acknowledge concerns honestly rather than overpromising or glossing over flaws. Use simple language and avoid real estate jargon.

## Messaging Examples

To students, say "Found a place you like? We will help you book it in under ten minutes." Do not say "Submit an enquiry form and our team will process your request."

To landlords, say "Get qualified student tenants without the hassle of screening." Do not say "List your property on our platform for maximum exposure."

For WhatsApp templates, say "Hey [Name]! Saw you enquired about [Property]. Want me to arrange a viewing this weekend?" Do not say "Your enquiry has been received. Reference number: 12345."

## Naming

The product is always "Ten&See" with an ampersand, never "Ten and See." Service tiers are Basic, Premium, and Concierge — avoid "Pro" or "Enterprise." Refer to students as "students" or "you," never "users" or "clients." Refer to landlords as "landlords" or "property partners," never "vendors."

## Content Pillars

Housing guides cover how to find, inspect, and rent student housing in Malaysia. Neighborhood spotlights feature Sunway, Subang, KLCC, and other areas. Student life content covers budgeting, moving tips, and roommate advice. Trust and safety content explains how we verify properties and warns about scams.

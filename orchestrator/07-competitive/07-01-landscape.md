# Competitive Landscape

> MOC: [[00-meta/MOC - Company]]

## Direct Competitors

| Competitor | Model | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Edge |
|------------|-------|-----------|------------|----------|
| **iProperty** | General property listings | Huge inventory, brand recognition | Not student-focused, scams common, no support | Student-specific curation |
| **Mudah.my** | Classifieds | Free listings, huge traffic | Unverified, chaotic, no booking flow | Verified properties, human support |
| **Carousell** | Peer-to-peer marketplace | Popular with students | Mostly resale, not rentals | Dedicated rental platform |
| **Uni official portals** | University housing boards | Trusted by students | Limited inventory, no tech | Better UX, more options |
| **Facebook Groups** | Community groups | Free, word-of-mouth | Unorganized, scams, no booking | Structured, safe, supported |

## Indirect Competitors

| Competitor | Model | Threat Level |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| Property management companies | End-to-end management | Medium (if they add student focus) |
| Co-living spaces (e.g., The Hatch, Upstairs) | Managed shared housing | Low (different price point, but trend to watch) |
| Airbnb (long-term) | Short-to-mid term rentals | Low (too expensive for students) |

## Market Gaps

1. **No one owns the student end-to-end experience** — from search to move-in
2. **Trust is broken** — students don't believe listings are real
3. **Support is nonexistent** — competitors are self-serve only
4. **International students are underserved** — visa, language, cultural barriers

## Positioning Statement

> For students in Malaysia who need safe, verified off-campus housing, Ten&See is the only platform that combines curated listings with human support throughout the entire booking journey — unlike generic property sites, we don't just list rooms, we help you find a home.

## Competitive Moat (To Build)

- **Trust:** Verified properties + landlord background checks
- **Service:** Human coordinators for viewings and bookings
- **Data:** Proprietary student preference + conversion data
- **Community:** Reviews, referrals, student network effects

## Monitoring

Track competitors monthly:
- New features launched
- Pricing changes
- Marketing campaigns
- Customer complaints (social media, reviews)
